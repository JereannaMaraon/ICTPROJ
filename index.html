What Is Fire Prevention Month?

It's always important to be aware about the fires that occur in our country, mainly because of the hazards that we potentially have but aren't aware about.

The Fire Prevention Month occurs during the month of March, this is supposed to spread awareness about fires and potentially save you and your family's life.



Ways to Prevent Fires
Always keep chargers/chords unplugged when not in use. Make sure to not overcharge or leave your devices unattended while charging, because it may overheat
Always be cautious around open flames, always keep distance and put inflammable items far.
Keep a fire extinguisher at home, so if you see anything, as little as a flame, you can set it off already. It's better safe than sorry.
Always be focused while cooking. Never leave your stove open unattended. Or if busy, keep stove off when doing something else.
